Emerald City
Supporters

The Emerald City Supporters (ECS) are the loud and proud Sounders fans you will see standing, chanting, and cheering for 90-minutes each game in the South end of CenturyLink Field.

The Cascadia
Rivalry

The Sounders' biggest rivals are the Portland Timbers and Vancouver Whitecaps. Each season the team with the most points in games amongst the others earns the Cascadia Cup.
